# CampusBite 🍽️
## The Future of College Dining

---

## 🎯 The Aim (The Problem)
- **The Issue:** The 12:45 PM - 1:45 PM break is chaotic. Long queues cause students to waste half their break just waiting for food.
- **The Solution:** A smart pre-ordering system that allows students to order food *from class* before a strict 12:00 PM cutoff.
- **The Result:** Eat immediately. Zero wait times.

---

## 🚀 Execution & Innovation
- **Built for Speed:** Designed and engineered from scratch within a 3-hour hackathon.
- **The Ecosystem:** 
  1. An immersive, mobile-first Web App for Students.
  2. A streamlined Order Dashboard for Canteen Staff.
  3. A live "Now Serving" TV Board for the collection counter.
- **Design Philosophy:** "Neon Pulse" aesthetic - Deep purples and electric blues to create a premium, high-tech vibe.

---

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
### Frontend (Lightning Fast UI)
- React, Vite, TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS (v4) & Framer Motion
- Glassmorphism UI Components

### Backend (Robust & Lightweight)
- Python & FastAPI 
- Jinja2 Server-side rendering
- SQLite (Ensures 0ms latency for on-campus networks)

---

## ✨ Features That Stand Out
- ⏰ **Strict Time-Gating:** Automated cutoff at 12:00 PM.
- 💪 **Health Mode:** One-click toggle revealing Kcal and Protein for every dish.
- 🛒 **Smart Cart Engine:** Integrated cutlery fees and a persistent floating mobile cart.
- 🎟️ **Token Verification:** Unique 3-digit order token system.
- 📺 **Live TV Sync:** Auto-refreshing boards for staff and students.

---

## 🌍 The Impact
- **Students reclaim 20-30 minutes** of their daily break.
- **Canteen Staff gain massive operational efficiency** knowing exactly what to cook *before* the rush hits.
- **Encourages healthier eating** through transparent calorie and protein tracking.
